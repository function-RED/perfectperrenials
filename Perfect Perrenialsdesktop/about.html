Perfect Perennials is an online educational collective dedicated to creating and spreading resources regarding the benefits of perennial agriculture. We seek to encourage gardeners and farmers alike to incorporate perennials to develop a healthy and sustainable ecosystem.
What are perennials?
A perennial is a plant that lives for two or more years. While perennials die out each winter, a strong rootstock allows them to regrow. Some plants, like herbs, live only a few years. Other plants, such as trees, will live for many years. Perennial is a term used to differentiate from annuals and biannuals.
What is
permaculture?
Permaculture is an agricultural system that focuses on perennial plants as a renewable natural resource which enriches and preserves the ecosystem. By providing information regarding perennials, encouraging an ecological mindset focusing on sustainability.
